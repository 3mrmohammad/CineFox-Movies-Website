Frequently Asked Questions

What is Letterfoxed?

Letterfoxed is a platform where users can watch movies, rate them, and share their opinions with others.

How can I submit my movie review?

You can submit your review by clicking on the “Add Review” button on any movie page and filling out the form.

Is there a membership or subscription?

No, Letterfoxed is completely free to use. You can create an account to save your reviews and ratings.

What should I do when I face a problem?

You can contact us at lf.complaint@contact.com. We’ll respond as quickly as possible.
